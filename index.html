Aviator Professional Strategy Guide

Expert techniques and safe gaming practices for optimal results

1. Identifying Risky Starts

If three or more consecutive odds below 2.0x appear during the signal period, immediately stop betting.

If additional blue (low) odds continue to appear, cease gameplay. Close the application and wait for at least 15 minutes before resuming.

2. High Odd Risk Timing

If a 10x+ odd appears within ±1 minute of the signal time, this indicates a High Odd Risk period. Avoid placing bets during these intervals.

3. SHA512 Hash Code Method

After obtaining the SHA512 Hash:

• Select the first round among the last three rounds.

• If it shows 3x or higher odd, it's considered reliable.

• If a "Try Again" message appears, discontinue use of that signal and generate a new one for verification.

4. Targeting High Confidence Odds

Whenever possible, place bets on 3x+ odds with 80%+ confidence levels.

Base your decisions on the Signal Trust Level indicator.

5. Precise Signal Timing (Second-Based)

Example: Signal = 10:30:47

→ Only play the subsequent round (e.g., 10:30:48).

• Skip rounds that are too close to the signal second.

• Note that the game's initial 10 seconds are not counted.

6. Signal-Based Betting Sequence (3 Rounds)

For each signal:

• Play the next three rounds, skipping any previous rounds.

• If you win in any single round, stop playing.

• Generate a new signal for another set of three rounds.

• If all three rounds fail, change the Hash and generate a new signal.

7. Understanding and Practice

Before implementing the signal system:

• Test several rounds to observe patterns.

• Identify whether wins typically occur in the first, second, or third subsequent rounds.

• Example: If most big wins occur in the third round, skip the first two and target only the third round.

8. Additional Professional Tips

• History Pattern Analysis – Identify and follow trends.

• Session Pauses – Wait 10-15 minutes after each win.

• Play Moderation – Limit to 3-5 signals per hour.

• Stable Connection – Ensure reliable power and internet.

• Signal Logging – Maintain records in JSON/Excel format.
