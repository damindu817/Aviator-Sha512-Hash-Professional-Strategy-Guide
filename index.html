Professional Aviator Game Strategy Guide

Expert techniques and safe gaming practices for optimal results

1. Identifying Risky Starts

When approaching the signal time:

If three or more odds below 2.0 appear, immediately stop betting.

If additional blue (low) odds continue to appear, completely stop playing.

If more odds below 2 continue to appear, completely stop playing. Exit the app and wait at least 10-15 minutes before analyzing the chart system again and restarting the game.

2. High Odd Risk Timing

If a +100× or higher odd appears within ±1 minute of the signal time:

Consider this as a High Risk Zone.

Avoid betting during such times.

3. Aviator SHA512 Hash Method

Calculating a signal correctly using Hash Code:

1. Select the first round from the last three rounds in Aviator History.

2. If this Odd value is 3× or higher, it is reliable.

3. Click it and copy the SHA512 Hash Code.

4. Provide it to Xtreme Aviator SHA512 Predictor and get your desired Signal by applying the required Odd.

5. If you receive a "Try Again" message, abandon that signal and obtain a new one.

4. High Confidence Odd Range

Whenever possible:

Bet using the 2× - 3× odd range.

Make decisions based on the Signal's Trust Level (understood confidence).

Target only Odds with 70%+ Confidence.

5. Accurate Signal Timing (Second-Based)

Example: Signal = 10:30:47

➡️ Only play from the subsequent round (10:30:48).

➡️ Skip the round included in the Signal second.

➡️ Avoid considering the Game Start Initial 10 seconds.

6. Signal-Based Game Sequence (3 Rounds)

For one Signal:

Skip the previous round.

Play the next three rounds.

If you win in any round, stop playing.

If all three rounds are unsuccessful, change the SHA512 Hash and create a new Signal before trying again.

7. Understanding and Practice

Before playing:

Observe several rounds.

Study the patterns and behaviors of the currently active Algorithm in the game.

Investigate:

→ Does the win come in the first? Second? Third?

➡️ Example: If most wins occur in the 3rd round, skip the first two and play only the 3rd round.

8. Additional Professional Tips

• History Pattern Analysis – Identify trends to analyze future game behaviors.

• Session Breaks – Pause for 10-15 minutes after every Win.

• Limited Play – Use only 3-5 Signals within 1 hour.

• Stable Connection – Your internet and power supply must be stable.
